about us

Lucy's Cake Shop is a family owned and operated business which has been providing great tasting, beautiful cakes for over 40 years. Our reputation has been built on our elegant and delicious Wedding Cakes, as well as our diversity of different themes for special occasion cakes... that taste great! For years, San Antonio's major corporations, hotels, churches, military bases, and families have called on Lucy's Cake Shop to provide them with their special occasion cakes. We can create cakes with special corporate logos, emblems, and specific insignias and personalized party cakes. Our cakes have become the centerpieces of many San Antonio festivities. We are a family tradition with many of our customers... an initial wedding cake order leads to baby shower cakes, to children's birthday cakes, to graduation cakes... and continues on!
